Storybound Privacy Policy

Version 2.0.0

1. Overview

Storybound ("the Platform") is a participatory literary universe authored under the imprint S. Tory Bound.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Authentication data
  • IP address and device metadata
  • Billing information (processed via Stripe or payment processor)
  • Interaction data (choices, prompts, selections)
  • Fortune transaction data
  • Usage analytics

We do not sell personal data.

3. AI Processing Disclosure

Your inputs and interactions are processed by AI systems to generate fictional narrative content.

By using the Platform, you consent to such processing.

Generated Narrative is platform-owned intellectual property.

4. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers (e.g., Stripe).

Storybound does not store full credit card numbers.

Payment processors handle billing information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Use of Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide access to the Platform
  • Generate narrative content
  • Manage Fortunes and subscriptions
  • Enforce platform policies
  • Improve system performance
  • Prevent fraud

6. Data Retention

We retain account and interaction data as long as necessary to operate the Platform, comply with legal obligations, and maintain narrative continuity.

Generated Narrative may be retained indefinitely as part of the Storybound Archive.

7. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable safeguards to protect user data. However, no system is completely secure.

8. User Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request:

  • Access to personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of personal account data

Deletion of account data does not revoke Storybound's ownership of Generated Narrative or licenses granted under the Terms of Service.

9. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to:

  • Maintain sessions
  • Track performance
  • Analyze usage trends

Users may manage cookies via browser settings.

10. International Users

By using the Platform, you consent to data processing in the jurisdiction where Storybound operates.

11. Changes to This Policy

Storybound may update this Privacy Policy at any time.

Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated version.
